Location:  Wade's Pond
                    962 Magnolia Ridge Road
                    Boutte, LA  70039





The Brouhaha will be held March 21st, 22nd and 23rd at Wade's pond in Boutte,Louisiana. We will have formal battling Saturday, Sunday, and Monday, and have pickup battles Friday afternoon the 20th if people are interested. First sortie will start at approximately 9:30 a.m. We will do at least 2 battles on Saturday and Sunday, and one on Monday. The pond is available on Friday for speed testing, battling, and shakedown cruises. When you arrive please do not park at the house. There is a small bridge to the right after you pass the house that leads to the pond and the parking area.


Sign-ups and Payment

All participants should be members of the IRC and have insurance through NAMBA. Please visit www.namba.com and join for 2015 if you have not already done so. If you have yet to register with IRC please visit IRCWCC Membership Page to sign up. Please allow 1-2 weeks for your registration to process. 

Please bring your NAMBA ID card as proof of insurance to the pond. Remember if you don't sign up with NAMBA you will NOT be covered in the event of an accident.

Registration is $30 before March 5th, $35 at the pond. Battlers under 16 get a special discounted rate of $15 each. If you are planning to come, regardless of whether you have sent Wade a check yet, please sign-up on the IRCWCC Sign-Up page. The address to send your payments to is:

Wade Koehn, 
962 Magnolia Ridge Road
Boutte, LA 70039


Amenities

CO2, drinks, toilet, and wash basin will be provided. We will also have power pond side. Wade has removed his would-be fruit orchard as well so we will be able to park and pit on-site adjacent to the pond - as we more or less did last year. Jeff has also volunteered to provide used BBs to all allied participants, so let him know before the first sortie if you need some.


Hotel Information

The hotel for the Brouhaha is the same as last year. 

Cypress Inn
12275 Highway 90
Luling, LA  70070
866-679-8006

 We have reserved 12 double rooms for a flat rate of $89.00 a night. There are 6 rooms downstairs and 6 rooms upstairs so if you need a lower level room please reserve early. All 12 rooms are doubles, so if you were planning on staying by yourself they may have singles available on the first floor past the 6 doubles we have reserved. When you call the hotel mention you are with Model Warship Combat to get our group rate and get a room from our block. You will have to provide credit card info etc. when you reserve the room.

On site camping is also available if you would like to stay pond side. Contact Wade directly if you would like to explore this option.
