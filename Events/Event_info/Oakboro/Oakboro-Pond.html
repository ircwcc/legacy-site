Oakboro District Park
Approximately 40 acres and includes a Tot Lot, horseshoe pits, a stocked pond (no fishing yet), and about a mile of walking trails as well as covered picnic shelters and restrooms.  There is also a state of the art Amphitheater, lighted ball fields, and a 1000' Nature Trail and Walking Trail Bridge. The picnic shelters, Amphitheater and Ball Fields can be reserved for your special events for a fee. 


Motel is the Quality Inn in Albemarle, NC which is 15 miles away (But an exceptionally easy drive)
