Seabourne Creek Nature Park
One hundred sixty-four (164) acre Nature Park with walking, jogging, and biking trails, wetlands, prairie restoration area, butterfly garden, restrooms, and a four (4) acre lake stocked with bass, perch and catfish. Also inside the park is the R.W. Lindsey Gazebo, which is 1,025 sq.ft. equipped with electricity and available for rentals.
