Dear IRCWCC members who were eligible to vote.

Here are the results of the 2005 voting on the Rules Proposals and
choices for Member at Large and Site of Next Year's National
Championship. Due to a minor number of respondents two of the rules
proposals were voided. With only 18 total members votes counted a 2/3
majority was not available on those proposals.

1.     PART V-SECTION A.-SUMMARY-

Add: #3. The numbers of campaign battles, their duration, and etcetera
shall be decided at the captain's meeting before NATS.

Was Approved by majority.

2.     PART II-SECTION B.-HULL PENETRABILITY.PARAGRAPH 2-

Add: [at the end of the first sentence]"and a second drop test
Wednesday morning."

Was Disapproved.

3.     PART II-SECTION J.-COMPLIANCE WITH CONSTRUCTION RULES-

Add Paragraph: All ships shall be placed on their sides and the area
of penetrable window be measured and verified, etcetera.

Was Disapproved for lack of a 2/3 majority.

4.     PART II-SECTION J.-COMPLIANCE WITH CONSTRUCTION RULES-

Add to Section 4: Prior to the start of NATS each Hull will float
within 1/8 inch of it's marked waterline, et al.

Was Approved.

5.     PART V-SECTION D. 1.-CONVOY SHIPS AND THEIR DUTIES-

Replace: "once in every 20 minute period" with "once in every 10
minute period".

Was Approved.

6.     Mr. Robert Stalnaker has been reelected as our Member at Large
and we all wish him a happy and productive service in the conduction
of that office.

7.     BYLAWS ARTICLE III. ELECTED OFFICERS; DUTIES. PARAGRAPH C.

Change the Second Sentence to: They shall serve from the first day of
January next following their election until the last day of December of
that same year or until a successor is duly appointed.

Was Approved.

8.     DATES AND LOCATION OF THE 2006 NATIONAL CHAMPIONSHIP

          Camp Conron, Holmes, NY     July 23 - 28, 2006

Thank you for taking the time to participate in the rules process of
the IRCWCC. Without our Combat Captain's Votes we are just another
model boat club. take pride in what we have and preserve it.

Bill G.
Club Secretary
