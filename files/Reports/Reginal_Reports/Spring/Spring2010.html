Spring Regional 2010

Well, the regional was scheduled early this year, April 16-18. The choice was brought on by a wish to go to an Ohio battle on Armed Forces day (with the hopes that some of them would make it out for our battle).

So.... Friday weather was great, temps in the high 60's or lower 70's, however, Saturday and Sunday were in the 50's and rather cool. This did not dampen the spirits of the combatants who battled through it all and enjoyed it all.

The allied forces could tell from the first battle in which three sank with in 4-5 minutes of the start of the battle and the going just got worse as the battles went by. The Axis could claim a hugh victory for this weekend with few of their numbers visiting Davy's Jone's locker.

Notable this weekend were:
A couple of rookies out for their first battle
Five Bis's (many sporting their new 7 unit strength.)
a three unit strong VDT squadron (so called the four horsemen but lacking one in number)
Good group from OAF joined us also!
