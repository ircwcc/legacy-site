Spring Regional 2004
Hagerstown, Maryland - May 21-23



A goodly Crowd was there!

On the dates of May 21-23, 2004, combat commenced between the Allied and Axis forces. Aided by the influx of powerful reinforcements from the southern states, the Allied forces won just about every battle during the regional.

Members from the FRAG (Florida Region Attack Group) attended this regionals and we were very happy to have them! It was great getting to know new friends and seeing old ones that have been out of touch for some time.
