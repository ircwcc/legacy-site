NE.Spring Regional 2008
Hagerstown, Maryland - May 9,10,11
Greenbriar State Park, Boonsbouro, MD

Friday morning was rather dismal, low clouds and light rain at 8 AM, heavier rain at 10 AM. I retired to Denny's and then the hotel at about 11. But... by 2 PM the rain had run it's course and we returned to the Lake and we had a good battle in the afternoon.

Highlight of this battle was the sinking of Plato's Yamato (twice) due to the impeller on his pump slipping. Whatever the reason, the Allied forces loved seeing it go down twice.

Saturday morning started with more rain and everyone went for a slow breakfast, and took their time in preparing this morning. The rain finally left and it was off to the lake again. This time we got in two battles with about 15 ships on the water.

Sunday was a cooler day but with no rain and we got in a single battle with many of the ships showing defects which need corrections before Nats. Regional has been a great success and everyone enjoyed the fellowship, and battling.
