Hull Busters Archive
Thanks to Brian Koehler from MWC for allowing us to share his access and founding member Fluegel for giving us permission to post here scanned images of the original combat model warship newsletter, Hull Busters. While some of the information here is dated (much of the early articles are posted for nostalgic sake and not current DIY information), there is still a lot of valuable information about our hobby, some of the technology, and of course the nutty humor of guys who play with model ships! So here they are :

Many thanks to Fluegel for getting all these digitized and sharing these articles with us again.
