In Memory of a captain that left us too soon Brian Spychalski
These Articles where taken from the pages of the premier News letter of its time Hull Busters. They cover information written about Brian during his short time with us in this hobby as well as letters written from the members in his memory.  May you never forget the time he spent with us and the life’s he touched the friends he made and the fun he had.
Brian was a senior at St John’s High school he was planning on attending Marquette University. He was active in the school Band as well as a Game club. He was also involved in a program where high school Students tutored children in Elementary school. Brian was well liked and an excellent Student and a great Captain
