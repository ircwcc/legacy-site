Steve Pavlosky brought this inflatable "alien" to 1998 NATS and we dubbed him the "Technical Representative from the planet AAXXIISS." 










Soon after the earlier photos were taken, and shortly after drinking from a Coke offered to him by a "friendly" Allied captain, our TechRep was struck down by a mysterious illness and died. He was buried with full military honors. 











At the banquet at the end of NATS it turned out that Cameron Moury had taken the most damage and survived in his USS California, so Steve presented the Alien to Cameron as an award and told him he had to bring it back the following year.












The inflatable doll only held up a year or two and then circa 2003 Nate & Brandon Graham designed the trophy version that is today's award. But it is still awarded each year to the captain whose ship took the most damage in a single battle and survived at that years NATS.
